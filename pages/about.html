About AI Book Sphere

AI Book Sphere is a simple digital bookstore website created for Project 1. It helps users explore books and receive basic recommendations based on their preferences.

What We Offer

  • Browse books by title, author, and genre
  • View a structured table catalog
  • Submit preferences through a form

Genres Available

Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery, Self-Help, and Non-Fiction (sample set). You can expand the list anytime.

How Recommendations Work (Simple)

On the Signup page, when a user selects a genre, JavaScript displays a short list of suggested books. This is a beginner-friendly approach that simulates “intelligent” recommendations without a database.
